(For foreign guests staying at our accommodation)

Welcome to Seoul! 🌆 Han Korea has a very strict waste sorting system. To facilitate compliance with the rules during your stay, we have prepared simple and easy-to-understand instructions for you. Please follow these steps, and the rest is up to us ♻😊

1️⃣ General Waste (일반 쓰레기  – Non-Recyclable)
🧻 Discardable:

small plastic packaging, dirty bags

used tissues, cotton swabs, hygiene products

chicken bones, large fish bones 🐔🐟

rubber gloves, sponge, toothbrush


🚫 Please don't throw trash outside yourself! The system in Seoul is relatively complex.
✅ Please do this:

Use indoor general trash cans

Separate from recyclables

🛎 When the trash can is full, please inform our staff so we can clean it up for you.


2️⃣ Food Waste (음식물 쓰레기  – Food Residues)
🥕 Discardable:

leftover food, noodles, bread, fruits, and vegetables

eggshell, coffee grounds ☕

Little Fishbone


🧯 Treatment method:

Food grinder using the kitchen sink

Turn on the faucet 💧, add the soft residue, and grind it.
⚠ Hard or fibrous waste (such as bones, shells, onion skins, corn husks) should not be thrown in to avoid damaging the machine!

Chicken bones and large debris → for general waste disposal


3️⃣ Recyclable Items (재활용  – Clean and Classified Items Only)
♻ Recyclable Categories:

Plastic 🧴: Bottles, Takeout Boxes (requires rinsing)

Glass 🍾: Bottles, jars (no food scraps allowed)

Paper 📦: newspapers, flattened cardboard boxes, clean cardboard boxes

Metal 🥫: Beverage cans, food cans (must be rinsed thoroughly)

Foam plastic 🧊: Must be clean and dry


🗂 Method of Handling:
🤢 If dirty or contaminated with food → considered general garbage, not recycling!
🛎 If the trash can is full, please let us know, and we will help you clean it up.

4️⃣ Large Waste (대형 폐기물  - Large Items)
🧳 Including:
suitcase, furniture, mattress, appliances

📞 Please contact the accommodation manager before discarding, and we will handle it for you.
🚫 Disposing of large amounts of trash will result in fines.

⚠ Notes
🚯 littering = fines 🧾
🚫 Do not mix recyclables, general waste, and kitchen scraps together.
♻ Please use the correct trash can
🙋♀ If you're unsure, feel free to ask us!
