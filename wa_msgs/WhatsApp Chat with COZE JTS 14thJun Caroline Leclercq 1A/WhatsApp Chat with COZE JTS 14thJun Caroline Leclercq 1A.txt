Welcome to Seoul!🌆 Korea has a very strict garbage separation system. We will guide you through the following procedures so that you can easily follow them during your stay. Leave the rest to us ♻😊

1️⃣ General garbage (일반 쓰레기 – incombustible)
🧻 You can put it in:

small plastic packaging, dirty wrapping paper

used tissues, swabs, and sanitary products

the bones of a chicken-the bones of a large fish 🐔🐟

RUBBER GLOVE, SPONGE, TOOTHBRUSH


🚫 Do not take out garbage directly. Seoul's system is complicated.
✅ Do this instead:

Use a general trash can in the room

separate from recycled waste

🛎 Please let the staff know when the trash can is full.


2️⃣ Garbage (음 식물 레기) – food residue)
🥕 You can put it in:

Remaining rice, noodles, bread, fruits and vegetables

eggshells, coffee kasu ☕

a small fish bone


🧯 Processing method:

Use a kitchen disposer

Drain 💧 Crush with soft residue
⚠ No hard or fibrous substances (bone, shell, onion skin, corn skin, etc.) are allowed. The machine is going to break!

the bones and large residues of a chicken → To the general garbage


3️⃣ Recycling (재활용) – Only those that are neatly separated)
♻ Recycleable:

PLASTICS 🧴: Bottle, takeout container (after washing)

Glass 🍾: Wine bottle, bottle (no leftovers)

Paper 📦: Newspapers, folded cardboard, clean paper boxes

Can 🥫: Beverage can, food can (wash well)

styrofoam 🧊: Clean and dry stuff


🗂 Processing method:
🤢 dirty or leftovers → It's regular garbage!
🛎 Please let the staff know when the trash can is full.

4️⃣ Large garbage (대형 폐기물 – large garbage)
🧳 For:
Suitcases, furniture, mattresses, home appliances

📞 Be sure to contact the accommodation manager before throwing it away.
🚫 If you leave it in a public place, you will be fined.

⚠ Precautions
🚯 Illegal dumping = Fine 🧾
🚫 Do not mix recycling, general garbage, or food waste
♻ Use the correct trash can
