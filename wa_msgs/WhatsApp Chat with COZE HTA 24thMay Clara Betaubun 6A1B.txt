1️⃣ Open the lid and check the packets
Inside, you’ll usually find:
noodles
dried vegetable flakes
black bean powder sauce
seasoning oil (sometimes olive oil)
2️⃣ Add the vegetable flakes first
Sprinkle the dried vegetables onto the noodles.
⚠️ Don’t add the black bean powder sauce yet.
3️⃣ Pour in hot water
Fill with hot water up to the inside line and close the lid.
⏰ Wait:
Regular cup: about 4 minutes
Large cup: 4–5 minutes
4️⃣ Drain the water carefully
Use the drain holes in the lid to pour out most of the water.
💡 Important tip: Leave a little water behind — about 3–4 spoonfuls.
If you drain everything completely:
your noodles may become dry and sad 😭
5️⃣ Add the sauce powder and oil
Now add the black bean powder sauce and seasoning oil.
Mix everything very well for about 20–30 seconds.
🔥 The more you mix, the shinier and tastier the noodles become.
